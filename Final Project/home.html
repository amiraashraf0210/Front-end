Explore Egypt's most iconic landmarks, from the Pyramids to the Nile.

Pyramids of Giza
History of the Pyramids
The Pyramids of Giza are one of the wonders of the ancient world, dating back over 4,500 years.
They consist of three main pyramids, the largest being the Great Pyramid of Khufu.
This iconic landmark represents an incredible engineering achievement in construction and design.
Nile River
The Magic of the Nile
The Nile River is the longest river in the world, serving as the lifeblood of Egypt.
It provides water and resources for agriculture and fishing.
The river flows through many historic cities and features stunning landscapes.
Karnak Temple
Wonders of Karnak Temple
Karnak Temple is one of the largest places of worship in history.
It features numerous massive columns and ancient hieroglyphs.
Tourists visit this historical site to enjoy its beauty.
Sphinx
The Legend of the Sphinx
The Great Sphinx is an iconic statue with a lion's body and a human head.
It has stood the test of time, watching over the Giza Plateau for centuries.
The Sphinx holds many mysteries and is a significant symbol of ancient Egypt.
